Plant Power
There is an abundance of scientific evidence showing that indoor plants, and plants in general, have numerous psychological and physiological benefits for the body.

Decrease of Cortisol Levels


Some studies have found that the presence of plants can improve the coritsol levels in the body. Melinda Knuth, an assistant professor of horticultural science at North Carolina State University, states that there is a connection between being around plants and the cortisol levels in the body. Cortisol levels, which is held in saliva, is decreased when in the presence of plants.
